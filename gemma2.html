Introducing Gemma 2 Swahili

üìÑ Kaggle ü§ó Hugging Face GitHub

We were really fascinated by the Gemma 2 Japanese releases last year, so much that we felt compelled to build Swahili variants of the models.

We are excited to announce Gemma 2 Swahili, a comprehensive suite of Gemma 2 language models specifically adapted for Swahili understanding and generation. This release is an important step toward bringing frontier AI technology to more than two hundred million speakers.

The checkpoints are live on Kaggle and Hugging Face. You will also find comprehensive documentation covering training, inference, and evaluation. Try the models and share your feedback.

Model Suite Overview

  • Gemma2 2 B Swahili Preview ‚Äì specialized continual pre‚Äëtraining for Swahili comprehension. A fully trained version is coming soon.
  • Gemma2 2 B Swahili IT ‚Äì lightweight instruction tuned model for efficient text generation.
  • Gemma2 9 B Swahili IT ‚Äì balanced instruction tuned model that offers a strong quality to compute ratio.
  • Gemma2 27 B Swahili IT ‚Äì flagship model that achieves state of the art Swahili understanding and task completion.

Key Capabilities

  • Native Swahili generation and comprehension
  • Advanced instruction following
  • High performance on technical and creative tasks
  • Cultural awareness of East African context
  • Efficient deployment from phones to servers

Example 1 ‚Äì Creative writing (Gemma 2 B Swahili IT)

Prompt
Tunga hadithi fupi kuhusu Twiga
Response
Twiga alikuwa mnyama mwitu wa asili ambaye alikuwa na furaha ya kuishi katika jiji la bandarini. Alikuwa amefungwa kwa nyumba yake ya kawaida, lakini kwa sababu ya uchovu wake, hakuwa na muda wa kucheza.

Hata hivyo, jiji lake lilikuwa likikumbwa na mabadiliko ya hali ya hewa. Maji yalikuwa yakipungua, na siku moja Twiga aligundua kuwa maji ya ziwa yalikuwa yametoweka. Hofu ilimvaa, kwa sababu kupotea kwa maji kungemaanisha kupotea kwa makazi yake.

Twiga alitamani kurudisha maji ya ziwa lakini hakujua aanzie wapi. Alijaribu kuuliza barabarani bila mafanikio. Baada ya muda alimuona mzee aliyeketi karibu na ziwa na akaomba msaada.

Mzee huyo alimweleza kuwa siri ya kurudisha maji ilikuwa kwenye mtihani wa kuunganisha nguvu za viumbe wote wanaoishi kandokando ya ziwa. Twiga alifuata maelekezo, akashirikisha wanyama na watu. Kufikia jioni, maji yalirudi na Twiga akagundua thamani ya ushirikiano. Alirudi nyumbani akiwa na furaha na shukrani kwa jamii yake mpya.

Performance Benchmarks

Swahili MMLU (Massive Multitask Language Understanding)

Fine‚Äëtuning on high quality Swahili corpora closes a decades‚Äëlong performance gap. The 2 B model improves from 15 percent to 34 percent accuracy, while the 9 B variant reaches 55.8 percent. Sentiment classification shows similar gains. The 2 B instruction model adds almost 18 percentage points over its base version. Larger models hover near the ceiling of the dataset, with the 27 B quantized checkpoint achieving 88.5 percent accuracy. These results highlight the value of targeted Swahili fine‚Äëtuning rather than relying on generic multilingual pre‚Äëtraining alone.
