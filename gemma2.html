Introducing Gemma 2 Swahili

üìÑ Kaggle ü§ó Hugging Face GitHub

We were really fascinated by the Gemma 2 Japanese releases last year, so much that we felt compelled to work on Swahili variations of Gemma 2 models.

We are excited to announce the release of Gemma 2 Swahili, a comprehensive suite of Gemma 2 language models specifically adapted for Swahili language understanding and generation. This release represents a significant step forward in making advanced AI technology accessible to the two hundred million plus speakers of the language.

All models are now available on Kaggle and Hugging Face, as well as comprehensive documentation on training, inference and evaluation. We are excited for you to try these models out and we look forward to your feedback.

Model Suite Overview

  • Gemma2 2 B Swahili Preview: A specialized 2 B continual-pretrained model with improved Swahili language understanding. A fully trained version will be released soon.
  • Gemma2 2 B Swahili IT: A lightweight instruction-tuned model optimized for efficient Swahili text generation.
  • Gemma2 9 B Swahili IT: A balanced instruction-tuned model offering enhanced capabilities while maintaining reasonable compute requirements.
  • Gemma2 27 B Swahili IT: Our flagship instruction-tuned model delivering state of the art Swahili understanding and strong task completion.

Key Capabilities

  • Native Swahili text generation
  • Advanced instruction following in Swahili
  • High performance on technical and creative tasks
  • Cultural context awareness for East African content
  • Efficient deployment options across scales
