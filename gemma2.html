Introducing Gemma 2 Swahili

📄 Kaggle 🤗 Hugging Face GitHub

We were truly inspired by the Gemma 2 Japanese release last year. That excitement pushed us to build Swahili variants of the Gemma 2 models.

Today we’re releasing Gemma 2 Swahili — a family of models specifically adapted for Swahili understanding and generation. This marks a major step toward bringing advanced AI to the 200 million‑plus speakers of the language.

All checkpoints are available on Kaggle and Hugging Face, together with comprehensive docs covering training, inference, and evaluation. We can’t wait to see what you build!

Model Suite Overview

  • Gemma2‑2B‑Swahili‑Preview – continual‑pretrained; a fully trained version is coming soon.
  • Gemma2‑2B‑Swahili‑IT – lightweight instruction‑tuned for efficient generation.
  • Gemma2‑9B‑Swahili‑IT – a balanced instruction‑tuned model with enhanced capabilities.
  • Gemma2‑27B‑Swahili‑IT – flagship model delivering SOTA Swahili understanding and task completion.

Key Capabilities

  • Native Swahili text generation & comprehension
  • Advanced instruction following
  • Strong creative & technical writing
  • Cultural context awareness for East Africa
  • Scalable deployment options (from phones to servers)

Performance Benchmarks

Swahili MMLU

Fine‑tuning unlocks a ~20–40 pp absolute gain across all model sizes, with the 9 B variant crossing the 55 % accuracy mark — outperforming comparable multilingual baselines.

Sentiment Analysis

On sentiment analysis the gains are equally dramatic: the 2 B model jumps from 49 % to 66 %, while the 27 B quantized model reaches ≈ 88 % accuracy — a new state‑of‑the‑art for Swahili.

Looking Ahead

Gemma 2 Swahili is just the beginning. We’re exploring speech models, cross‑lingual transfer, and even lighter on‑device variants. Feedback, pull‑requests, and benchmark results are all welcome — let’s push Swahili AI to the frontier together!
