Nadhari is an artificial-intelligence research and product lab. Our mission is to advance frontier AI research and applications in Sub-Saharan Africa (and beyond).

Africa is lagging behind in the AI race. We lack the infrastructure and capital needed to reach the frontier.

The good news is that knowledge is not scarce, and we have enough technical talent to push us through.

This is our only bet.

We are going to achieve our mission by:

  1. Conducting and open-sourcing frontier AI research on current and future architectures.
  2. Building and deploying products that apply this research at scale.

We aim to unlock a tremendous amount of value across the continent through intelligence augmentation.

This is an exciting challenge and opportunity.

It’s an honor to play a part in building an abundant world.
